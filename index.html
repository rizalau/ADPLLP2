Our Leader


Arjana Bagaskara Solichin

Founder & Managing Partner


Arjana is a co-founder and managing partner of ADP. He was born in Berlin, Germany. He feels to be borne as a lawyer. He has more than 5 years of experience as a civil and commercial dispute lawyer. He holds an LLB degree from Padjadjaran University in Bandung, and candidate for a master of law in business law from Pelita Harapan University in Jakarta. He was a criminal defense lawyer of a public figure, Vanessa Angel, the deceased when she was accused of owning a psychotropic, Xanax. As an Indonesian commercial litigator, he represents a wide variety of foreign companies, Indonesian companies, and local business owners in their interests in Indonesia, with an emphasis on insurance, bankruptcy, and commercial disputes. He practiced commercial disputes for over 5 years in many jurisdictions, such as the Indonesian National Board of Indonesia (BANI), ICC Arbitration Rules, and the London Arbitration. Besides as a lawyer, he has been appointed as a legal expert to assist the Ministry of Investment/Investment Coordinating Board (BKPM) on investment realization.
